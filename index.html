about me

-- Developer--
--UX / UI Designer--

I was born in Czech Republic but grew up in London, England. I am a problem solving enthusiast with particular interest in the world of computing. As I spent much of my time in London, my passion for computing continued to exponentiate which played a great deal in finding my way to New York University. At New York University, I specialised in Computer Science, Design and Business. In my Junior year I took a semester at University College London specialising in Innovation Science and Management as well as, a semester at La Sorbonne in Paris studying History and French.

I am now a Full Stack Developer living in San Francisco. I am familair in particular with Ruby, Javascript, CSS and HTML, SQL and Rails.(A longer list of skills can be found in my resume) My true passion lies in backend development however, having previously worked as a UX/UI designer I am able to understand the importance of building a user friendly application.
