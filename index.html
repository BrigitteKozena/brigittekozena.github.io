Web Developer --

I studied Computer Science and Design at NYU. Straight after graduating I completed 1000+ hours of coding through the Dev Bootcamp Program. I find myself most experienced in Ruby, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, HTML & CSS and SQL. I also am familiar with many other languages/frameworks and have the ability to learn new skills quickly. Throughout my time at DBC I have built a number of miniture version of infamous sites such as Twitter, Amazon and Craig's List, as well working on my own Passion Projects. I have had experience working in teams/peer pairing, using the git workflow and am familiar with software development methodology and release processes.

-- UX / UI Designer

Having worked previously in UX/UI Design I understand the importance of building user-friendly applications. It is very easy to loose site visitors due to the lack of design and object placement upon a page. While writing my code I always think about how the user will interact with the site rather than just implementing the pure functionalities. In addition, I have experience working with Adobe Suite and building wireframing/workflows.

about me

I was born in Czech Republic but grew up in London, England. I am a problem solving enthusiast with particular interest in the world of computing. As I spent much of my time in London, my passion for computing continued to exponentiate which played a great deal in finding my way to New York University. At New York University, I specialised in Computer Science, Design and Business. In my Junior year I took a semester at University College London specialising in Innovation Science and Management as well as, a semester at La Sorbonne in Paris studying History and French.

I am now a Full Stack Developer living in San Francisco. I am familir in particular with Ruby, Javascript, CSS and HTML, SQL and Rails.(A longer list of skills can be found in my resume) My true passion lies in backend development however, having previously worked as a UX/UI designer I am able to understand the importance of building a user friendly application.
