Project: Cameo Body Suit

A theoretical project I developed at NYU that was aimed at the future of Fashion. The concept is a cameo body suit that allows you to change outfit with the click of a button. It allows you the ability to go straight from a suit, to gym clothing and then evening attire without having to stop and change
