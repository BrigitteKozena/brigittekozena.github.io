Blankenship Dry Goods


Role: Web Developer


Skills: Photoshop, Balsamiq, Photography, HTML, CSS, Javascript

Blankenship Dry Goods is an online fashion clothing company. As a web developer, my duties were to be part of a team that developed their e-Commerce website. However, depending on what stage of development the site was at my duties varied. When images of the company’s products were needed, my position was shifted from a Web developer to a photographer.

Essential Job Duties:

    - Development of the company’s e-Commerce website.
    - Development of wireframes and e-Commerce site prototypes.
    - Assisting in research and development of the company’s products profiles.
    - In-house photograph shooting for product images.
